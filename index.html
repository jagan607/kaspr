How do we work?

One of the frameworks that clients love and keep coming back to is as shown below. Broadly, We have 4 phases


  • Ideation and Analysis

  • Development

  • Deployment

  • Maintenance

When you come to us with your requirements for any product or services, We try to understand the pain point that has made you come to this solution. We try to understand the various applications/ services currently in use and if there is something we can add to it rather than building everything from scratch, making it more cost-effective for you.


Our ideation phase helps you and us in :

    Cost-effective approach

    Add a new feature that you didn’t think of, which in the future will help you when you scale your business.

    Understanding the target audience to design a more classic/modern UX/UI.


Once we are clear on what we need to build. We first provide you with samples of different UI, if you don’t already have them.

We, at Kaspr, follow Agile methodology i.e we get back to you every 2-3 weeks to show the progress and make necessary changes and align ourselves to each other. We follow a TDD (Test Driven Development) approach to ensure that, unlike most other companies, we don’t compromise on the quality of the product. With everything going digital we are aware of the vulnerabilities that lie.

Our products go through rigorous testing rounds like Unit Testing, Integration Testing, Regression Testing. Smoke Testing, Performance, and Load Testing, Security Testing, and of course the Beta/Acceptance testing.


Once we are done with the development, we hand over the application and the source code to you.

As per the non-disclosure agreement that we sign, We will neither be talking about your idea nor keeping a source code of your product with us. We will help you get your product up and running and will also provide COMPLIMENTARY 4-week support so that you don’t have to worry about any surprises coming up after the product delivery.

We are a team with conviction, once you choose to work with us, we make sure everything you had in your mind is out there in the real world. We treat your product as our own and deploy none but our best resources to get the job done!


Now, this is an optional step, but often we have seen customers wanting to hire us for maintenance and enhancements for their products and solutions.

It is the kind of product delivery that we do that they wouldn’t go to anybody else.

We’ll not say much about this last phase, You’ll understand this once you start working with us!
